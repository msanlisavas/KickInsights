# KickInsights Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 12, 2026

## Overview

KickInsights is a browser extension that estimates viewer counts on Kick.com by analyzing chat activity. It is designed with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

**KickInsights does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data generated by the extension is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API and never leaves your browser.

## What Data is Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device only:

- **Session history**: Timestamps, viewer count snapshots, and chat statistics for streams you have tracked
- **Calibration profiles**: Per-channel participation rates learned from census results
- **User settings**: Participation rate, rolling window size, overlay preferences
- **Overlay position**: Position and size of the on-page widget

## What Data is NOT Collected

- No personal information (name, email, account details)
- No chat message content (only usernames are counted, messages are not stored)
- No browsing history
- No cookies or tracking identifiers
- No analytics or telemetry
- No data is sent to any server, API, or third party

## Chat Message Processing

The extension intercepts Kick.com's WebSocket messages to count unique chat participants. Only usernames are extracted and counted in memory. Message content is never read, stored, or transmitted. Username counts are aggregated into anonymous statistics (e.g., "352 unique chatters") and the individual usernames are discarded when they leave the rolling time window.

## Data Retention

- Session data is automatically pruned after 30 days
- Users can manually clear all data or per-channel data at any time via the extension settings
- Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

## Third-Party Services

KickInsights does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.
